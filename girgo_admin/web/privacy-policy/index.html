Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Girgo collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Girgo mobile application and related services.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as name, email, phone number.
  • Profile and delivery information such as address details.
  • Order, subscription, and transaction related information.
  • Technical information required for app functionality (for example, device and notification token data).

How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process orders, subscriptions, and payments.
  • To provide customer support and service notifications.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and security.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with trusted service providers (for example, payment, authentication, or messaging providers) only as needed to run app features.

Data retention and deletion

You can request account deletion from within the app. For details, see Account Deletion.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@girgo.in.
