Delete your Girgo account

You can permanently delete your Girgo account and related account data directly from the app.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Girgo app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.
If re-authentication is required by Firebase security rules, sign out, sign in again, and retry deletion.

What gets deleted

  • Your account authentication record.
  • Your profile data stored by Girgo.
  • Your user-linked records associated with the account, where supported by current app logic.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app, email us from your registered email address at support@girgo.in and request account deletion.
